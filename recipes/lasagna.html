The best lasagna recipe from grandma!

These are the ingredients:

  • 1 pound lean ground beef
  • 1 medium onion, chopped
  • 2 teaspoons minced garlic
  • 1 (29 ounce) can tomato sauce
  • 1 (6 ounce) can tomato paste
  • 1 ½ teaspoons salt
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 16 ounces shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 12 ounces cottage cheese
  • ½ cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 (12 ounce) package lasagna noodles
The following steps are relevant:
  1. Step 1Cook ground beef, onion, and garlic in a large skillet over medium heat until the meat is browned.
  2. Step 2Add tomato sauce, tomato paste, salt, and oregano and stir until well combined and heated through.
  3. Step 3Stir mozzarella, cottage cheese, and Parmesan together in a large bowl.
  4. Step 4Spoon a layer of the meat mixture onto the bottom of a slow cooker.
  5. Step 5Add a double layer of uncooked lasagna noodles, breaking noodles to fit into cooker as needed.
  6. Step 6Top noodles with a portion of cheese mixture.
